About Me:

I am a senior research scientist at Criteo Labs. Before Criteo, I worked at Verizon lab for cyber security and mobile advertising related projects. I received my M.S and Ph.D from Duke University in 2010 and 2015. My Ph.D research focused on machine learning, and my advisor was Lawrence Carin.
